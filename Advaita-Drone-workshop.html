Proposal for Code Studio: Drone Workshop at Advaita 2025, IIIT Bhubaneswar

Greetings from Team Advaita IIIT Bhubaneswar!

We are beyond excited to propose a collaboration that we believe would mutually enhance and benefit both your organisation and Advaita 2025, IIIT Bhubaneswar's Techno-Cultural Festival.

Recognizing Advaita's established reputation as one of the premier festivals in Odisha and the country, drawing in around 15,000 students from all over the country, we believe that a drone-focused workshop from your esteemed organisation would be an innovative and engaging addition, perfectly aligning with the festival's technological focus.

The "Code Studio: Drone Workshop" is designed to provide practical experience and in-depth knowledge about drone technology to students attending Advaita. Participants will gain hands-on skills in drone operation, basic programming for drones, understanding drone mechanics, and applications of drones.

The workshop shall feature:

  • Expert instruction from 2-3 experienced drone specialists.
  • Around 80-100 participants.
  • Interactive sessions allowing students to actively work with drones.
  • Introduction to the theory, basic concepts, and foundational knowledge of drone technology.
  • Practical exercises and demos showcasing real-world applications of drones.
  • The organisers are expected to NOT charge any registration fee from the workshop participants.

We believe that this workshop will attract a significant number of students interested in robotics, aerospace, and technology in general, further boosting Advaita's draw and reputation as a hub for innovation.

We will be providing the following facilities:

  • Venue: A suitable space to conduct the workshop, including an indoor area for instruction and a safe, designated outdoor area for drone flying demonstrations.
  • Publicity and Promotion: Promoting your esteemed organization across Advaita's official communication channels to maximize audience reach and engagement.
  • Logistical Assistance: Support with travel and accommodation, registrations, participant management, and other basic facilities.
  • Complimentary Passes: Providing complimentary Advaita passes for the drone workshop experts facilitating the event.

We are excited to host the "Code Studio: Drone Workshop" as part of Advaita 2025, set to take place from March 28th to March 31st, 2025. We request you to share your logistic and other requirements to ensure the successful conduction of the event. We are attaching the tentative timeline; please let us know any changes required.

We are eager to discuss this proposal further and answer any questions you may have.

Thank you for your time and consideration. We look forward to the possibility of collaborating with you to make Advaita 2025 an even greater success!
